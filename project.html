Our Project

Our project focuses on evaluating the impact of compression techniques on large language models (LLMs). Specifically, we examine Meta's LLaMA model and apply knowledge distillation as our compression method. The objective is to assess the feasibility of knowledge distillation for reducing the size and energy consumption of LLaMA, providing insight into how LLMs can be optimized without significant loss of performance.
